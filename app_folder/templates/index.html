Hi 👋 Welcome to Bookle,

where ebook searching is made easier.


Instead of needing to order or borrow a physical copy of a book, Bookle helps you find the online version of the book by searching it up for you (so you don't have to type it more than once).


The free-to-read sources that are currently searched by Bookle are:

  • EpubLink
  • Open Library
  • San Jose Public Library

The pay-to-read sources that are currently searched by Bookle are:

  • Google Play Books
